About Hallies Travels

Hallies Travels Limited is a private limited liability company registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission(CAC) in the year 2016. We are a passionate company who specialize in creating personalized experiences for families, couples, and adventurers alike. With a wealth of knowledge and personal experiences at popular destinations such as Miracle Garden, Disneyland, Wild Wadi, and more, we are dedicated to making your dream vacation a reality. At Hallies Travels, we understand the importance of creating lifelong memories.
